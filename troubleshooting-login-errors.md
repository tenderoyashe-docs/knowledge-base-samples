# Troubleshooting Login Errors

## Summary
Use this guide if you cannot log in even though your password is correct.

## Symptoms
- “Invalid username or password” message
- Login page refreshes without logging in
- Account appears locked

## Checks
1. Confirm you are using the correct email address.
2. Try resetting your password.
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try again.
4. Try an incognito/private browser window.
5. Try a different browser or device.
6. If using a VPN, disconnect and try again.

## If your account is locked
Wait 15 minutes and try again. If the lock persists, contact Support to unlock the account.

## When to contact Support
Contact Support if:
- You still cannot log in after completing the checks above
- You are not receiving password reset emails
- You suspect your account has been compromised
